Top 200 drugs to memorize
Current for 2014 / 2015

A pharmacy technician will want to know as many top 200 drugs' names and their FDA approved uses as possible. If you're working in a pharmacy, an internship, on the job training, or a pharmacy tech program you should follow what your program requires. Typically, memorizing the Top 200 drugs list is recommended. Either way, refer to your program's list.
